Exercise 03

What's next for you?

Which of these feels most like your natural next move?

Reading the label
Jordan has been trying to eat better lately — less processed food, more home cooking. He's been buying organic occasionally and noticing labels more. He came across one that mentioned how the animals were kept, and found himself reading it when he normally wouldn't have.
What the fuss is about
Jade's flatmate has been talking about factory farming recently. Jade hasn't really engaged — it's not something she thinks about much. But she found herself googling something her flatmate mentioned, just to see what the fuss was about.
The numbers surprised him
Dan signs the occasional petition when something comes up — climate, NHS. He got sent one about farming standards by a friend. He almost didn't read it, but the numbers in it surprised him. He clicked sign without really thinking about it.
