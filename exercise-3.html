Exercise 03

What would you do?

Read the scenario below.

Scenario

You are heading to work on a Monday morning when you notice a pigeon on the pavement.

She isn't moving much and she's struggling - just sitting there while people step around her. One wing is dragging on the ground.

A few minutes later the street cleaner comes past with his cart and sweeps the bird in with the rubbish on the pavement. You watch it happen.

How does this make you feel?

Is there anything you might do about this?
